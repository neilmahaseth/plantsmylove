DIY Durable UV Resistant Plastic Pot

  • Home
  • DIY Projects
  • DIY Durable UV Resistant Plastic Pot
  • September 13, 2019October 16, 2021
  • by Neil Mahaseth

In this DIY project, I will show you how I converted a normal plastic container into a UV Resistant Plastic Pot. The materials used for this project are cheap and easily available.

▼ Materials @ Used ▼

  • Plastic Container
  • Aluminum Foil
  • Cello Tape
  • Metal Rod
