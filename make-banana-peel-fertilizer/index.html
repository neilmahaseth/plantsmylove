How to make Banana Peel Fertilizer

  • Home
  • DIY Projects
  • How to make Banana Peel Fertilizer
  • March 4, 2022March 16, 2022
  • by Neil Mahaseth

Hi, friends in this video I will show you how we prepare banana peel fertilizer for our flowering plants. This is a very strong fertilizer as all the nutrient is instantly available to the plants. So use it in small quantity and sufficiently diluted in water. This fertilizer created is rich in potassium and should be applied to flowering plants. In a plant that is about to flower, this will increase the number of blooms and their size also.
